### Decentralized Messaging Platforms

1. **Whisper (Ethereum)**  
    A peer-to-peer encrypted messaging protocol designed for secure communication between Ethereum dApps.
    
2. **Waku**  
    Successor to Whisper, optimized for scalability and privacy in Web3 messaging.
    
3. **Matrix (Element)**  
    An open decentralized communication protocol supporting encrypted messaging and voice/video calls.
    
4. **Session**  
    A privacy-focused decentralized messenger built on the Loki network with no phone number required.
    
5. **Status**  
    A decentralized messaging app and Web3 wallet built on Ethereum.
    
6. **Secure Scuttlebutt (SSB)**  
    A decentralized, offline-friendly social and messaging protocol.
    
7. **Briar**  
    A peer-to-peer encrypted messaging app that works over the internet, Tor, or Bluetooth.
    
8. **Tox**  
    A fully decentralized and encrypted messaging platform with no central servers.
    
9. **Ricochet**  
    An anonymous messaging platform that uses the Tor network.
    
10. **IPFS + libp2p Messaging**  
    Messaging systems built on IPFS and libp2p for decentralized peer-to-peer communication.
